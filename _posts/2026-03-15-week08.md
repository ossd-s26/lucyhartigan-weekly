## Other teams work ... and other reflections

I am really glad we are doing weekly standups in class. Even though we did project evaluations frequently, I still am not entirely familiar with every project that each group will be contributing to, and its been great to hear more about it. I especially like hearing about the projects and the work from the perspective of people who find it the most interesting ( or ideally that is how the groups and selections worked out! ). So far, we've only had one standup and it seems like most groups were still on the set-up phase. I know my group was! One of the groups, working on keycloak, seemed to have a lot of progress making contributions which I loved hearing about. Keycloak was a project I was unfamiliar with before this semester, but I remember Ben mentioning how he wanted to work on it earlier in the semester.
